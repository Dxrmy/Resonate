**Resonate** is an intelligent mental health and journaling platform designed to turn your data into emotional clarity. By integrating Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and Stoic philosophical frameworks, it helps you understand the signals your mind is sending.

## Key Features
- 🧠 **Journaling with Context**: Multi-modal journaling designed around CBT/DBT practices.
- ⚖️ **Stoic Guide**: Integrated philosophical reflection tools to build resilience.
- 📈 **Mood Analytics**: Yearly visualization of your mental states to identify triggers and progress.
- 🔍 **AI Insights**: Local LLM-driven analysis to provide personalized insights on your entries.
